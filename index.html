How to Play the Piano

  • Keys: Click on the piano keys or use your computer keyboard to play notes.
  • Instrument: Select an instrument from the dropdown to change the sound.
  • Volume: Adjust the volume using the slider.
  • Octave: Use the Octave +/- buttons to shift the range of notes.
  • Record: Press the Record button to start recording your notes.
  • Playback: Play back your recorded notes using the Play button.
  • Clear: Clear your recording with the Clear button.
  • Mobile/Laptop Mode: Switch between Mobile and Laptop modes for the best experience.
  • Preset Songs: Select a song from the dropdown and press Play to hear it.
